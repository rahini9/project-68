Welcome to the VIBGYOR Alumni Association (VAA), alumni of VIBGYOR Group of Schools, a leading chain of K-12 Schools in India. VIBGYOR Group of Schools is an institution built on the dream to honour young people by giving them an environment to grow intellectually, artistically, athletically and morally.

The primary objective of the VAA is to promote and maintain contacts among the former-students of VIBGYOR Group of Schools and to help the school with matters concerning its promotion, development and welfare.

Objectives

The VIBGYOR Alumni Association (VAA) aims to promote and foster mutually beneficial interaction between the Alumni and the Alma Mater; to encourage the Alumni to take abiding interest in the progress and development of the Alma Mater; to give former-students of VIBGYOR High an opportunity of helping one another and of expressing their loyalty to their school by rendering assistance as and when occasions arise; and to exchange professional knowledge, organise conferences, seminars workshops, training courses etc.
